About The Topic

The "Structure and Function in Living Organisms" topic in IGCSE Edexcel Biology provides a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental biological principles that govern the form and function of living organisms. This subject delves into the intricacies of how living organisms are structured, how they function, and how these aspects are interrelated.

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this topic, students should be able to:

  • Describe the structure and function of cells and their components.
  • Explain the processes involved in the movement of substances, nutrition, respiration, transport, excretion, and coordination.
  • Understand the interdependence of organ systems in maintaining life.
  • Apply biological principles to real-world situations and experimental contexts.
